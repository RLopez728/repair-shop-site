ABOUT US

Hundred Plus

Hundred Plus is a small, home-based computer repair service focused on straightforward and reliable repairs. This is an independent operation, started small and built around careful, hands-on work rather than high-volume service.

Because the shop is home-based, service is offered through scheduled pick-up and drop-off only. This allows us to keep costs low and focus entirely on diagnosing and repairing your device.

Hundred Plus is a dream first, and a computer repair shop second.

The goal is simple: practical repairs, fair pricing, and clear communication.
